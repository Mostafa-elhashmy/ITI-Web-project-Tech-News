Foundations of Digital Defense

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks,
and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are
usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive
information; extorting money from users; or interrupting normal
business processes. A career in cybersecurity is crucial and
ever-evolving, requiring a diverse skill set to combat
the increasingly sophisticated threats in our digital world.


Our comprehensive track starts with the basics, ensuring you
build a strong foundation before diving into advanced topics.
You'll learn about information security processes, threat
landscapes, common vulnerabilities, and defensive strategies that form the core of cybersecurity expertise.

Certification Roadmap

CompTIA Security+

Foundational certificate that assesses basic necessary skills to perform core security functions and act as a springboard for intermediate-level cybersecurity roles and cybersecurity.

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)

This CEH program teaches aspiring cybersecurity professionals to think like a hacker to better defend against them. It covers ethical hacking process, footprinting and reconnaissance.

Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP)

A hands-on, practical certification that requires candidates to successfully attack and penetrate various live machines in a safe lab environment, highly regarded by security experts.

Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)

An advanced certification that requires candidates to possess expertise in designing, implementing, and managing best-in-class cybersecurity programs, requires relevant experience requirements.
